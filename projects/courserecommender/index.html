UMSI Course Recommender System

Team Members: Eric Koo, Sanmeet Kanhere

UMSI offers many courses, but come registration time it is always a pain to find good classes to take. Using a dataset that contained all of UMSI's past course registration history we designed a course recommender system that allows students to input what classes they have already taken in order to get recommendations based on what other students with similar schedules have taken. We are currently part of the UM Learning Analytics Fellowship Program and received a grant to continue building and improving the system.
