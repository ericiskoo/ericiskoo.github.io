AATA Mobile App Redesign

Frustrated by the poor usability of the current Ann Arbor city bus app, I decided to do a redesign as a project for my Introduction to Interaction Design class. The process included brainstorming, concept mapping, storyboards and scenarios, wireframing, paper prototyping, and culminated in a final interactive medium-fidelity prototype created using Axure.
