Hi. I'm Eric.

I'm interested in using data to inform decisions and derive insights and strive to put information to good use through visualizations and solid interface design. Mostly I just enjoy working on and building things that people will find useful. I have lots of experience dealing with large amounts of data and also have strong front-end development skills.

I am currently a graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Information Analysis and Retrieval (IAR). I did my undergrad at UCLA and double majored in Cognitive Science and Statistics.

I guess I'm a nerd. In my spare time I play the computer game Dota 2 (I enjoy the stats). I really like Pokemon. I enjoy playing soccer and I used to go running more often, but I've been slacking off (but in my defense it has been pretty cold this winter in Michigan, especially compared to home in California).
